Computational estimation of the impact of mutations on protein-ligand affinity based on Alphafold and MD simulation

University of Pisa



***In progress***

1. Workflow

Generate amino acid sequence of mutation proteins from the FASTA file of wt protein retrieved from Protein Data Bank

Predict the 3D structure of the proteins by AlphaFold2

Fix protonation state of 3D structures by H++ server

Align the predicted structures with the crystallographic model by Chimera

Separate ligand(s) from crystallographic structures and add hydrogens with Obabel then prepare with GAFF2

Convert pdb file of predicted protein structures in isolation to .gro format

Assemble protein and ligand

MDS

Solvate

Neutralize by adding Na+ or Cl-

Minimize energy

NVT for 1ns

NPT for 1ns

Simulate for 15ns

Compute binding free energy by gmx_MMPBSA

Exclude outlier(s) and compute the average
